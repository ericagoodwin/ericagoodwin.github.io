Hi! I’m a 3rd year PhD student at the University of Chicago, researching computer science education with Diana Franklin and the CANON lab. I'm interested in helping students feel better and do better in college-level theory courses.

My current project evaluates how personally-relevant curriculum can impact student performance and engagement in the Algorithms class. Broadly, I care about improving equity and access within CS and STEM education as a whole.

Previously, I graduated from Harvey Mudd College with a B.S. in CS/Math. During my undergrad I developed curriculum and taught CS for some great organizations including the Prison Education Project, STEAM Coders, and Girls Who Code.
